Architecture

How It Works

A single, lightweight pipeline runs entirely on a standard smartphone — from camera frame to spoken answer in seconds.

01

Input

The phone camera captures a high-resolution frame of the target currency note.

02

OpenCV Processing

The image is automatically rotated to landscape mode, cropped to isolate the center, and standardized.

03

ML Classification

A Scikit-learn Random Forest classifier matches the extracted visual DNA against the trained currency dataset.

04

Audio Output

Text-to-speech announces the result, built on TalkBack and VoiceOver accessibility principles.

Impact & Advantages

Independence, by design.

Built around one of life's highest-risk daily tasks — and engineered to reach everyone who needs it.

Economic Security

Independently confirm the denomination of every note. No more being short-changed, handed fakes, or relying on a stranger's honesty.

Absolute Privacy

Processing can happen locally. No images need to be stored or sent to external corporate clouds. Your financial context remains strictly your own.

Runs On Any Phone

No special hardware, no physical add-ons. Built to run efficiently using a camera that is already in the user's pocket.

OS-Agnostic & Cheap

Designed to be compatible with Android TalkBack and iOS VoiceOver. Cost-efficient and accessible to all devices.
